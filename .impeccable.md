## Design Context

### Users
Prospective affiliates — developers, agencies, and digital entrepreneurs who want to partner with Marblism and earn recurring commissions by referring clients to the platform.

### Brand Personality
Professional, sober, and functional. Language is direct and jargon-free. No hype, no filler. Calm confidence with an electric undertone — the brand feels dependable and sharp at the same time.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone & theme**: Dark mode. Deep near-black backgrounds with high-contrast text and electric accent highlights. Minimal chrome; let content breathe.
- **Color palette**:
  - Background primary: `#0c0c14`
  - Background surface: `#14141f`
  - Accent electric: `#5b5ef4`
  - Accent hover: `#4f46e5`
  - Border subtle: `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`
  - Text primary: `#f1f5f9`
  - Text secondary: `#94a3b8`
- **References**: Minimal dark SaaS aesthetic — clean grid, restrained use of color, utility-first layout.

### Site Purpose
Affiliate landing page for Marblism. Goal: convert prospective partners into signed-up affiliates by clearly communicating the program's value and providing a single, frictionless call-to-action.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — every element earns its place. Remove anything decorative that doesn't aid comprehension.
2. **Calm foundation, electric edge** — the baseline is quiet and structured; accent color is used sparingly to direct attention.
3. **Functional hierarchy** — headline → benefit proof → CTA. No detours.
4. **Restrained motion** — subtle hover transitions only; no auto-play animations.
5. **Typographic discipline** — one font family, limited weight range, consistent scale.
